System Performance: Finding the Weakest Link

Common Bottlenecks:

  • Too little RAM: System uses slow storage as backup
  • Slow storage: Long load times, stuttering
  • Weak GPU: Low frame rates in games
  • Old CPU: Everything feels sluggish
